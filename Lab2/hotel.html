City Park Hotel

Overview

The City Park Hotel is situated on the edge of the beautiful Phoenix Park and enjoys a stunning location in Dublin City. We offer a luxurious experience and all the amenities that the discerning guest would expect.

Facilities

Our hotel is of 5-star standard. We have beautifully furnished and spacious rooms, an award winning restaurant, a convivial resident’s bar, and outdoor grounds of outstanding quality. Our facilities are second to none!

Rooms

Each room is decorated in an individual style in keeping with the varied and tumultuous history of the Phoenix Park. Many special event, concerts and murders have occurred in the park over the years and our designers have allowed this rich history to influence the décor. The "Pope’s Cross" room, for example, features a real cross.
